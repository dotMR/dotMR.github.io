Looks good albeit a bit flat, crust is better, flavor is bland. This was the last of a batch of dough.Â To avoid the excessive crustiness of last time I baked a little cooler and shorter.

  • Formed the loaf and let it rise for 3 hours, upside-down in a glass bowl.
  • Baked 20 minutes @ 475 (after preheat).
  • Dropped temp to 425, rotated loaf, baked for another 20 minutes.
  • Turned oven off. Opened oven door to release some heat, let loaf in oven for 15 minutes.
