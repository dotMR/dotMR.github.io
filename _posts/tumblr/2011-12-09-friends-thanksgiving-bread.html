A couple of loaves for Ryan’s annual Friends’ Thanksgiving

310g Water

20g Yeast

(hydrate for 20 minutes)

400g APWF

75g RF

25 WWF

(marinate for 20 minutes)

17g Salt

kneaded thoroughly

Once kneaded I let this rise in the car on the way to NYC and then left it outside on the porch overnight.

Ran into some complications during cooking (Tur-Duc-Ken drippage) and I was forced to take the bread out early after a flare up. The crust got a little charred on the bottom, but overall the flavor was great.

Baked for 20 minutes @ 500 degrees, 15 minutes @ 350, then out.
