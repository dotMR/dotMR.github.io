Brain bread? Octopus bread? I forgot to score the dough before baking.

New recipe:

  • Replaced 1c (cup) APF (All Purpose Flour) with 1/2c WWF (Whole Wheat) + 1/2c RF (Rye Flour)
  • Reduced the yeast to 1.25 tbsp
  • Rise for 2 hours
  • Knock-down, flipped the dough
  • Rise again for 2 hours
  • In the fridge overnight
  • Let this loaf rise for 90 minutes before bake @ 435 for 40 mins.Â
