Back at it. This is the second loaf of the batch and they both turned out pretty well.

Dough:

625g Water

15g Yeast

— Let sit hydrate for 15 minutes —

At this point I decided to make smaller batch

Using 300g of the mixture above, combine:

450g APWF

50g RF

— let sit for 15 minutes

add 15g Kosher Salt

— let sit for 15 minutes

knead

— let the dough rise for 2 hours, then refridge —

Bread:

preheat oven to 475

form loaf

— let rise for 60 minutes while oven is heating stone —

put loaf in oven, spray walls of oven with water

— 20 minues @ 475 —

rotate loaf

— 20 minutes @ 425 —

— turn oven off, remain in oven for 20 minutes —
