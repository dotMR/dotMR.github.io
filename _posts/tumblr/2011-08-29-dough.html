Made a batch last night:

——————————-

1000g Flour

[ 850g All Purpose Flour ]

[ 75g Rye Flour ] 

[ 75g Whole Wheat Flour ]

600g Water

15g Active Dry Yeast (Hydrated for 10 minutes)

30g Kosher Salt

——————————-

  1. Combine water + yeast, let hydate for 10 minutes.
  2. Add flour, salt to hydrated yeast in stand mixer.  Mix briefly to combine, let sit for 30 minutes.
