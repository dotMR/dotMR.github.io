Started another small batch of gelato. Â Trying to find a baseline recipe for small batch by weight gelato.

——————————-

500g Whole Milk

75g Sugar

Cornstarch slurry (instead of egg yolk)

[ 100g Whole Milk ]
[ 20g Cornstarch ]

25g Basil leaves

Zest of 1 lemon

——————————-

Mix up slurry until Cornstarch dissolves (prob lower the # CS next time)

Combine basil, zest, sugar, whole milk in sauce pan and heat until it starts to bubble.

Add slurry, simmer for a couple minutes, cool completely (fridge overnight).
