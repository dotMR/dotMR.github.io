I’m not happy with this loaf. It looks fine, but it just doesn’t have the taste or texture I’m looking for.

I cut the yeast in this batch of dough, but this loaf came out smelling like alcohol..which I would expect from too much yeast. Also, I only got the pockets (holes) that I wanted on the outer extremities - the inside was pretty dense.
