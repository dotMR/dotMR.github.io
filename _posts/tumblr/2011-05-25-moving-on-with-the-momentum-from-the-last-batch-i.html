Moving on with the momentum from the last batch I baked another loaf right away.  I’m disappointed with the flavor of the last loaf - sort of bland.  Crust turned out well though.  This loaf I let proof for what amounted to 3 hours, which was too long.  You can see the loaf kind of fell.

I changed the bake time a bit in response to the super-crustiness of the last loaf.  this one was baked for 15 minutes @ 475, then 20 minutes @ 425 and another 15 minutes with the oven off.  The inside came out a bit moist though.  It needed to be baked for longer.
